About this site

Why Morbid Analytics?

The human brain is catastrophically bad at estimating risk. We fear plane crashes and shark attacks while ignoring the slow, undramatic, statistically inevitable causes that actually kill us.

This site exists to stare mortality in the face with a spreadsheet. Every cause of death is real, every number is sourced, and every entry on the absurd end of the spectrum is a genuine documented case — because the universe, it turns out, has an extraordinary sense of humor.

Built as a Quarto-adjacent project using vanilla HTML, CSS, and Chart.js — the kind of thing you do when your distributed systems assignment is due and you decide to do something completely different instead.

🎓 Academic context

This site was submitted as a Quarto website assignment for a data science course. The goal: demonstrate markdown, theming, and data visualization skills. The execution: a complete descent into mortality statistics. I regret nothing.
